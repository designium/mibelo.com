Explain to me Mibelo's logo.

The logo is an inspiration from many different sources. The first one is my previous project called Fortunica which is a social fortune telling network.

The second one is Google. After working there for three years, I just wanted to use colors some what related to Google's logo.

Finally, The block divisions of colors are meant to represent steps of purchase funnel. Mibelo missions is to decrease those steps as much as possible.
- Chim Kan, founder
